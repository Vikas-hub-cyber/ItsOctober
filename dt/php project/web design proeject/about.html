About Us

With over 40 years of experience in the automotive aftermarket, Shrestha Automobile(SA) is a leader in the after-market parts business in Nepal. We are involved in vehicle spare parts distribution ranging from Heavy Commercial Vehicles to Two Wheeler segment.
Shrestha Automobile(SA) provides superior products and services to their valued customers at competitive prices while delivering the best customer service in the industry. Located at the heart of the city,Shrestha Automobile(SA) occupies over 8000 sq. ft., of warehouse and office space, holding large stock of vehicle spare parts suitable for all types of vehicles.
Shrestha Automobile(SA) boasts more than 1000 valuable retail customer spread all over Nepal. Our widespread network and reputation among customers is what differentiates us from our competitors.
We at Shrestha Automobile(SA) represent a strong collective energy, wisdom and collaborative efforts that empower our organization in which individuals have the knowledge, skills, desire and the opportunity to personally succeed in a way that leads to collective organizational success.
